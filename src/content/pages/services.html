How We Can Help You

Pacific Prosperity Financial works with two types of clients: people who hold mortgage notes and want to sell them, and investors looking to grow their wealth through note investments. Whatever side of the table you are on, we are here to make the process simple and transparent.

For Note Sellers

Turn Your Mortgage Note Into Cash

If you hold a mortgage note — whether you created it through seller financing, inherited it, or acquired it another way — you do not have to wait years for monthly payments to trickle in. Pacific Prosperity Financial connects you with qualified buyers through our extensive investor network, so you can convert your note into a lump sum of cash on your terms.

Our brokerage approach works in your favor. Instead of dealing with a single buyer, we market your note to multiple investors who compete to give you the best price. We handle everything from evaluation to closing, typically completing transactions in 30-45 days. There are no fees or hidden costs to you — we only get paid when your note sells. Whether you want to sell the full note or just a portion of the remaining payments, we will walk you through every option in plain language so you can make the best decision for your situation. Not sure where to begin? Our step-by-step selling guide covers the entire process.

For Investors

Earn Real Estate Returns Without the Hassle

Mortgage note investing lets you earn attractive, real-estate-backed returns without the headaches of property ownership. No tenants to manage, no maintenance calls at midnight, no vacancies to worry about — just consistent, passive income secured by real property. It is one of the most overlooked opportunities in real estate, and we make it easy to get started. New to notes? Learn the fundamentals in our complete guide to mortgage notes.

Pacific Prosperity Financial gives you access to a curated selection of performing and non-performing mortgage notes across residential, commercial, and land properties nationwide. Whether you are a seasoned investor looking to diversify your portfolio or exploring note investing for the first time, we provide thorough due diligence, transparent pricing, and the guidance you need to invest with confidence.
