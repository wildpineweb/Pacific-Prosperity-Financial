About Pacific Prosperity Financial

Pacific Prosperity Financial was founded by Tim Joslen, who brings over 35 years of experience in the financing and real estate industry. An entrepreneur at heart, Tim started his first business at a young age and has always been driven by a passion for helping others succeed. In 2025, he made the decision to pursue mortgage note brokering full-time, dedicating himself to bridging the gap between note holders seeking liquidity and investors looking for quality opportunities.

What sets Pacific Prosperity Financial apart is our commitment to integrity, transparency, and client education. We believe that every transaction should be fully understood by all parties, which is why we take the time to walk you through every detail. Whether you are selling a note or investing in one, you can count on us to be available, responsive, and dedicated to achieving the best possible outcome for you.

Our reputation has been built one satisfied client at a time. We understand that trust is earned through consistent results and honest communication, and that is exactly what we deliver. Pacific Prosperity Financial is proud to be a trusted partner in the mortgage note industry, helping note holders unlock the value of their assets and connecting investors with opportunities that align with their goals.

My goal has always been simple: help my clients succeed. Whether you are looking to sell a mortgage note or invest in one, I am here to guide you every step of the way with honesty, expertise, and a genuine commitment to your success. Tim Joslen Founder
