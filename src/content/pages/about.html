About Pacific Prosperity Financial

Pacific Prosperity Financial was founded by Tim Joslen, who brings over 35 years of experience in real estate and financing. An entrepreneur at heart, Tim started his first business at a young age and has always been driven by a passion for helping others succeed. He founded Pacific Prosperity Financial with a clear purpose: to help note holders who want to convert their mortgage notes into cash, and to connect investors with quality, real-estate-backed opportunities — all with the honesty and transparency that every client deserves.

Most note holders are not industry insiders. They created a note through seller financing, inherited one, or ended up with a note through other circumstances — and now they want guidance from someone they can trust. That is exactly where Tim thrives. He takes the time to explain your options in plain language, ensures you understand every detail of the process, and works to get you the best possible outcome. For investors, Tim provides access to vetted note opportunities and the thorough due diligence needed to invest with confidence.

I understand that selling a mortgage note or making an investment can feel overwhelming. My job is to make it simple, fair, and completely transparent — so you always know exactly where you stand. Tim Joslen Founder
